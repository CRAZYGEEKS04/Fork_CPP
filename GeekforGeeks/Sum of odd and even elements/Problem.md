Given an integer N, find the sum of odd numbers and even numbers from 1 to N.

Example 1:

Input: 5

Output: 9   6

Explanation: Odd numbers upto 5 are 1, 3, 5 and their sum = 1 + 3 + 5 = 9.

Even numbers upto 5 are 2 and 4 and their sum = 2 + 4 = 6.
